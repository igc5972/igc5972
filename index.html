Research

Motivating Research Interests
Studying assembly of stellar mass for intermediate redshift galaxies (0.5 < z < 4; "Cosmic Noon"). Analyzing resolved property maps of galaxies constructed through pixel-by-pixel SED fitting by making non-parametric measurements. Investigating the role of internal star-formation vs. external star formation (e.g., from mergers) and the impact of each on galaxy growth.

galaxy evolution; galaxy mergers and interactions; moderate-redshift; high-redshift; high performance computing; SED modeling; computer vision

JWST Surveys
Using JWST data from CEERS, PRIMER, NGDEEP, and COSMOS-Web on the NIRCam instrument.

CEERS Photometric Catalogue
I am leading the efforts for the official NIRCam + HST photometric catalogue for the CEERS collaberation. Publication in prep!
